# Character
Constituion Tank-Tank character
A tank that takes damage for the team and manipulates terrain.  
Non-flying
Non-swimming

## Resource bar
Instances of damage grants resource bar.

## Passive
All friendly units in an AoE around this character will redirect a percentage of the damage taken to this character.  
Can be toggled to only include player characters, instead of all friendly units.
Can take fatal damage.
Attacks can grant multiple stacks.
Damage types transfer.
Damage done by teammates is not redirected. 

## Basic Ability
Slam the ground to deal damage and a stun in an AoE around character.

## Basic Ability
Create friendly terrain units that blocks all projectiles and movement. Can be destroyed and is effected by passive. 
Multiple options to shape of terrain. Created terrain decays after X turns. 

## Basic ability
Pick up terrain. Activate again to throw at a tile to deal damage and trap. Thrown terrain is considered a friendly unit and has stats.  
### Units and terrain units
Units standing inside terrain units are rooted unless they are ghosted or flying. All projectiles and autoattacks are redirected to the terrain unit. 

## Basic Ability
Gain a shield and take reduced post-mitigation damage. 


## Ultimate
Permanently destroy connected terrain, up to X tiles.
