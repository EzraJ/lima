# Character
Constituion Tank-Tank character, aptly named "Tank-Tank"(for now) 

## Resource bar
Instances of damage grants resource bar.

## Passive
All team units in an AoE around this character will redirect 20% + 1% per level will be redirected to this character. 
Past level 80 damage, friendly units are invincible but Tank-Tank takes more damage.   
Can be toggled to only include player characters, instead of all friendly units.  

## Basic Ability (Mid% max resource cost)


## Basic Ability (Mid resource bar, physical damage, High cooldown) 
Activating this ability gives a % increase in constitution(scaling with resource bar stacks), and causes Tank-Tank to slam the ground around it dealing damage scaling with constitution.
Consitution buff decays over X turns. 


## Ultimate (100% Current Resource bar, High cooldown)
Tank-Tank gains a shield that is a % of his maximum hp. The percentage is the same percentage that his resource bar is. 
For example, if his resource bar is at 50%, he will gain a shield that is 50% of his maximum HP. If his resource bar is 100%, he will gain a shield that is 100% of his health bar.
