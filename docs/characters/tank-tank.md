# Character
Constituion Tank-Tank character
A tank that frontlines and takes damage for the team. 
Non-flying

## Resource bar
Instances of damage grants resource bar.

## Passive
All friendly units in an AoE around this character will redirect a percentage of the damage taken to this character.  
Can be toggled to only include player characters, instead of all friendly units.
Can take fatal damage.
Attacks can grant multiple stacks.
Damage types transfer.

## Basic Ability
Slam the ground to deal damage and a stun in an AoE around character.

## Basic Ability
Grant a shield to every unit effected by passive. 

## Basic Ability
Create terrain that can be destroyed and decays after x turns. Units cannot fly or dash over this terrain.
Blocks vision, projectiles, and non-melee auto attacks. 

## Ultimate
Gain a massive shield and take reduced post-mitigation damage(pure damage uneffected).
