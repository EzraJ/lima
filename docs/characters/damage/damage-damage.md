# Character
Charisma Damage-Damage Character

## Resource Bar
3 different resource bars, each corresponding with a type of damage.
Dealing pure damage fills up pure damage bar, same with other types of damage.


## Passive
Instead of the usual APT interactions, this character attacks once per turn, and damage is multiplied the APT stat.
For example, having an APT stat of 0.75 allows the character to autoattack, but has a ratio of 75%. Similarly, having an APT stat of
2.5 means this character can only attack once, but that attack has a ratio of 250%. 

# Physical Damage 

## Basic Ability
Spew out a blast of fire in an arc in front of the character, dealing physical damage.
